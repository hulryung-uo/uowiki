Papua is the **Lost Lands**' tropical port — a humid settlement on the eastern T2A coast,
serving as the gateway to the savage jungles beyond. Where [Delucia](/world/delucia/) is a
walled mining camp, Papua leans seaward: a harbor town where boats put in, hunters re-supply,
and the jungle presses close at the edges. It is the more full-featured of the two Lost Lands
towns, with a broad spread of shops to outfit expeditions into one of the most dangerous regions
in the realm.

## Getting there

- **Center:** (5770, 3180) — on the **east coast of the Lost Lands** (T2A), north of
  [Delucia](/world/delucia/)
- Like all of the Lost Lands, Papua has **no public moongate**. Reach it via the **dungeon and
  passage network** linking T2A to the mainland, by **boat** along the Lost Lands coast, or by
  [*Recall*](/skills/magery/) / *Gate Travel* to a marked rune. See
  [Moongates & shrines](/world/moongates-and-shrines/) and [Dungeons](/world/dungeons/).
