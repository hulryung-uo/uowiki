Delucia is a frontier town — a small, walled settlement in the **Lost Lands** (the T2A
territory), surrounded on every side by hostile country. Its walls are not decoration: outside
them roam the dangerous creatures of the Lost Lands, and Delucia exists as the safe harbor in
the middle of all that peril. Within the gates you'll find a bank, a smith, and the supplies a
hunter or miner needs to push back out into the wilds. For adventurers working the Lost Lands,
Delucia is base camp.

## Getting there

- **Center:** (5242, 3978) — in the **south-east Lost Lands** (T2A), well east of the
  Britannia mainland
- The Lost Lands have **no public moongates**. Reach Delucia through the **dungeon and
  passage network** that connects T2A to the mainland, or by [*Recall*](/skills/magery/) /
  *Gate Travel* to a marked rune. See [Moongates & shrines](/world/moongates-and-shrines/) and
  [Dungeons](/world/dungeons/) for the connections.
