Magincia was once the richest and proudest city in Britannia — and its pride was its undoing.
The old island city was laid to ruin, and what stands today is **New Magincia**, raised humbly
from the rubble. It is the City of Humility, and it wears the Virtue honestly: a modest island
settlement of farmers, fishers, and plain folk who remember what arrogance cost their
forebears. Quieter and smaller than its legend, New Magincia is a pleasant, out-of-the-way stop
reached by its own moongate, with a community-built feel and views of the surrounding sea.

## Getting there

- **Center:** (3728, 2164) — an island in the **east-central** sea, north-east of
  [Ocllo](/world/ocllo/)
- **Magincia Moongate** (3563, 2139) sits just west of the city — take any public gate and
  choose Magincia. Otherwise the island is reached by boat. Mages can
  [*Recall*](/skills/magery/) to a marked rune. See
  [Moongates & shrines](/world/moongates-and-shrines/).

A fisherman, mapmaker, and shipwright cover the island's seafaring trades, alongside a smith,
tailor, cook, and tavern keeper. For a fuller market, the mainland cities are a short moongate
hop away.

The rebuilt city is also home to the **Magincia Bazaar** — rentable merchant stalls clustered
near the center of town — and a **housing lotto** that periodically awards the island's open
building plots. Both run on Trammel and Felucca alike.
