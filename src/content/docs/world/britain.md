and home to Lord British's castle.

## Services

Per `world_knowledge.yaml`, Britain has the fullest set of services of any city:

bank · blacksmith · carpenter · tinker · healer · inn · mage shop · tailor · provisioner

Landmarks include **Lord British's Castle** (1323, 1624), **Blackthorn's Castle** to the north,
and the city **graveyard** (1380, 1490) — a traditional first hunting ground.

## Who should come here

Everyone, eventually — but especially **new characters**. Every craft trainer and supply vendor
is within walking distance, the bank is the realm's de facto marketplace, and the surrounding
countryside (graveyard, north forest) offers low-risk hunting and gathering. When in doubt,
start in Britain.
