If Britannia has a heart, it beats in Britain. Lord British raised his castle here, and the
realm grew up around it: this is the capital, the largest and most crowded city in the land,
and the place every road and moongate seems to lead back to. Where other towns pick a trade
and master it, Britain does a little of everything — craft halls and supply shops crowd
together along its streets, the bank steps double as the kingdom's marketplace, and adventurers
of every stripe pass through. For most newcomers it is simply *home*: the place you learn the
game, stock up, and return to between travels.

## Getting there

Britain sits at the center of the **Felucca/Trammel** mainland. The **Britain Moongate**
(1336, 1997) lies just south of the city walls — step through any public moongate and choose
Britain to arrive here free and instantly. Mages can [*Recall*](/skills/magery/) or *Gate*
straight to a marked rune; on foot, every major road on the mainland eventually feeds into the
capital. See [Moongates & shrines](/world/moongates-and-shrines/) for the full network.

## Who should come here

Everyone, eventually — but especially **new characters**. Every craft trainer and supply vendor
is within walking distance, the bank is the realm's de facto marketplace, and the surrounding
countryside (graveyard, north forest) offers low-risk hunting and gathering. When in doubt,
start in Britain.
