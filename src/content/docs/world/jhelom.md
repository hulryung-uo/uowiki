## Who should come here

**Fighters in training.** The fighting pits and weapon vendors make Jhelom the thematic home of
[Swordsmanship](/skills/swordsmanship/), [Tactics](/skills/tactics/), and the other combat
arts; the healer is conveniently close to the pit for obvious reasons. Fishermen also work the
surrounding sea. Being an island city, everything arrives by moongate or boat — keep that in
mind before buying in bulk.
