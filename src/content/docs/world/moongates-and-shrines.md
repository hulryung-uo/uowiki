Step into the blue light and Britannia shrinks to the size of a menu. Public moongates are the
realm's free travel network; the shrines are where the Virtues (and resurrection-seeking ghosts)
are honored.

## The moongate network (Felucca/Trammel)

Nine public moongates connect the mainland and islands. Coordinates per
`world_knowledge.yaml` (sourced from `servuo` `PublicMoongate.cs`) and the
[interactive map](https://uomap.vercel.app):

| Moongate | Location (x, y) | Serves |
|----------|-----------------|--------|
| Britain | (1336, 1997) | The capital — see [Britain](/world/britain/) |
| Minoc | (2701, 692) | [Minoc](/world/minoc/) and [Vesper](/world/vesper/) |
| Yew | (771, 752) | [Yew](/world/yew/) and the north-west forests |
| Trinsic | (1828, 2948) | [Trinsic](/world/trinsic/) and the south |
| Skara Brae | (643, 2067) | [Skara Brae](/world/skara-brae/) (gate is on the mainland side) |
| Jhelom | (1499, 3771) | [Jhelom](/world/jhelom/) and the southern isles |
| Moonglow | (4467, 1283) | [Moonglow](/world/moonglow/) island |
| Magincia | (3563, 2139) | New Magincia island |
| Buccaneer's Den | (2711, 2234) | The pirate haven — **no guards beyond the gate** |

The map data also lists a **Haven Moongate** (3450, 2677) near New Haven, plus gates in Malas
(**Luna** (1015, 527), **Umbra** (1997, 1386)) and Tokuno (**Makoto-Jima** (802, 1204)).

Using a gate is free and instant — double-click, choose a destination. For a mage,
*Recall* and *Gate Travel* offer point-to-point alternatives (see [Magery](/skills/magery/)).

Ilshenar has its own shrines at Valor (496, 380) and Justice (860, 600) per the map data.

Shrines matter practically: ghosts can be **resurrected** at a shrine by chanting the Virtue's
mantra, and the Virtue system ties activities to specific shrines. (Shard-specific Virtue
mechanics are not yet documented here — unverified.)
