Step into the blue light and Britannia shrinks to the size of a menu. Public moongates are the
realm's free travel network; the shrines are where the Virtues (and resurrection-seeking ghosts)
are honored.

![Map of Felucca moongates and shrines](/img/maps/felucca-moongates-shrines.png)

The map data also lists a **Haven Moongate** (3450, 2677) near New Haven, plus gates in Malas
(**Luna** (1015, 527), **Umbra** (1997, 1386)) and Tokuno (**Makoto-Jima** (802, 1204)).

Using a gate is free and instant — double-click, choose a destination. For a mage,
*Recall* and *Gate Travel* offer point-to-point alternatives (see [Magery](/skills/magery/)).

Shrines matter practically: ghosts can be **resurrected** at a shrine by chanting the Virtue's
mantra, and the Virtue system ties activities to specific shrines. (Shard-specific Virtue
mechanics are not yet documented here — unverified.)
