Where do you go to find a given monster? This page maps the **overworld** (above-ground)
spawns by region — what roams the countryside around each town, what lives in which terrain,
and the tougher creatures of the Lost Lands. It's built from the shard's own
`Spawns/felucca.xml` (Trammel mirrors the same overworld spawns). For **dungeon** spawns and
their bosses, see [Dungeons](/world/dungeons/).

## Around the towns (good for newcomers)

Step outside a town and you mostly meet **harmless wildlife** — boars, sheep, cows, chickens,
crows, eagles, rabbits — ideal first targets while you learn to fight. A few towns have a
signature threat just outside, though:

- **Trinsic & Minoc** — **ettins** roam the nearby hills; a real (but beatable) early fight.
- **Serpent's Hold** — **alligators** along its marshy island shores.
- **Cove** — **acid (toxic) elementals** nearby — bring cure.
- **Wind** — **corpsers and air elementals** around the mountain pass.
- **Moonglow** — mostly boars, but the odd **lich** or **spectre** wanders the island.
- **Yew & Skara Brae** — pastoral: sheep, boar, the occasional skeleton.

So a fresh character can grind animals right outside any town, then graduate to the ettins near
Trinsic/Minoc.

## The open wilderness, by terrain

Out in the countryside between towns, what spawns tracks the **terrain**:

- **Plains & farmland** — **boars**, sheep, cows, great harts, and other game (the most common
  overworld spawn by far).
- **Coastline & water** — **sea serpents** off the coasts, with **alligators** in shallows and
  swamps.
- **Forests** — **great harts, brown/black bears, timber wolves**, and harpies on the edges.
- **Swamps & bogs** — **alligators, corpsers**, and slimes.
- **The snowy north** — **polar bears** and cold-themed wildlife.
- **Mountains & passes** — **ettins, harpies, air elementals**, and the occasional troll.
- Wandering everywhere in smaller numbers: **mongbats, giant spiders, harpies**, and at night,
  undead like **skeletons and spectres** near graveyards.

If you just want a fight on the road, the mainland wilderness is thick with **boar, ettin, sea
serpent (near water), air elemental, and corpser** spawns.

## The Lost Lands (step up the danger)

The **Lost Lands** (reached through dungeon passages and certain moongates, east of the main
map) is a tougher overworld. Alongside hinds, brown bears, rats and crows you'll meet
**gazers, giant ice serpents, imps, alligators**, and — dangerously — roaming **ophidian
matriarchs**. It's the natural next zone once town-edge animals and mainland ettins are too
easy, and it surrounds several Lost Lands dungeons (Fire, Ice, Terathan Keep, Khaldun).

## Dungeons

The real concentrations of dangerous, valuable monsters are **underground**. Each dungeon has
its own themed spawn (and often a [champion spawn](/playing/champion-spawns/)) — Shame's
elementals, Destard's dragons, Deceit's undead, and so on. Those are documented per-dungeon,
with the exact creature lists, on the **[Dungeons](/world/dungeons/)** page.
