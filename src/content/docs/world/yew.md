Yew is less a town than a forest with buildings in it — a scattered woodland settlement of
lumberjacks and carpenters, seat of the Courts of Truth.

## Location

- **Center:** (546, 992) — the region sprawls roughly from (92, 656) to (756, 1261)
- **Nearest moongate:** Yew Moongate at (771, 752), in the forest north-east of town

## Nearby resources

- **Vast forests all around** — the Yew woods (roughly 100–750 × 650–1260) are the single
  largest [lumberjacking](/skills/lumberjacking/) area in Britannia. Trees everywhere, in
  every direction.

## Who should come here

**Lumberjacks, carpenters, and bowyers.** Nowhere matches Yew's tree density, and the carpenter
and bowyer shops buy what you make from the wood. Just plan your banking: with no bank in town
(per the region data), serious harvesters either work near the moongate for quick trips or
carry a strong back. The forests are also good low-key hunting for new fighters.
