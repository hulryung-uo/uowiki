Cove is a small, walled village wedged between the mountains and the sea, north-east of
Britain. It has no Virtue, no great guild, and no grand building — just a handful of cottages, a
bank, and a few honest shopkeepers getting by in the shadow of the nearby **Orc Fort**. What
Cove lacks in size it makes up for in convenience: it sits within easy reach of Britain, Vesper,
and the orc lands, which makes it a quiet waypoint for travelers and a favorite base for hunters
who like their excitement close to a bank.

## Getting there

- **Center:** (2236, 1210) on the Felucca/Trammel mainland, north-east of Britain
- There is **no Cove moongate**. The usual approach is by road from Britain (north-east) or
  from [Vesper](/world/vesper/) / the [Minoc](/world/minoc/) area to the north, or by
  [*Recall*](/skills/magery/) to a marked rune. See
  [Moongates & shrines](/world/moongates-and-shrines/) for the nearest public gates.
