Serpent's Hold is a fortress before it is a town — a stout keep on a southern island, garrisoned
by the **Order of the Silver Serpent** and dedicated to martial discipline. Where Jhelom is a
rowdy fighter's island, Serpent's Hold is the disciplined one: a place of training halls,
weapon racks, and knights who take their duty seriously. The walls keep the southern seas at
bay, and within them a surprisingly complete set of trades supports the garrison. Adventurers
come for the martial atmosphere, the safety of the keep, and the launch point it offers into
the wild southern waters.
