Trinsic, the City of Honor, hides behind high sandstone walls in Britannia's far south — a
fortified knight's city and the home of the paladin order. Honor is its Virtue and its
temperament: the streets are orderly, the guards vigilant, and the great gates a comfort to any
traveler who has just crossed the wild country around it. Within the walls you can train, heal,
re-supply, and rest in safety; beyond them lie jungles and mountains that test exactly the
martial virtues the city prizes.

## Getting there

Trinsic guards the **south** of the Felucca/Trammel mainland. The **Trinsic Moongate**
(1828, 2948) sits just outside the south wall — take any public gate, choose Trinsic, and walk
in through the gates. Roads run north toward Britain; mages can [*Recall*](/skills/magery/) to
a rune. See [Moongates & shrines](/world/moongates-and-shrines/).

## Nearby resources

- **Forest to the west** for lumber and game.
- **Jungle to the south** — wilder territory, with tougher creatures for hunters working up
  from beginner spawn.

## Who should come here

**Warriors with an honest streak.** Trinsic is the natural base for melee fighters — smith,
healer, and bank inside guarded walls, with escalating hunting grounds outside them. The
dragon dungeon **Destard** lies to the north-west across the mountains, making Trinsic a
staging town for ambitious (or foolhardy) dungeon parties. Tamers also pass through on the way
to the southern jungles.
