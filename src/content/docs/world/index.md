## Facets

Five facets are mapped (per `uomap/js/data.js`):

| Facet | Size (tiles) | Character |
|-------|--------------|-----------|
| **Felucca** | 7170 × 4098 | The facet of danger — full PvP. Reds are restricted here (`Config/General.cfg`). Better loot (see [server rules](/shard/server-rules/)). |
| **Trammel** | 7170 × 4098 | Same geography as Felucca; consensual PvP only. |
| **Ilshenar** | 2304 × 1600 | Land of the gargoyles. |
| **Malas** | 2560 × 2048 | Shattered land of Luna and Umbra. |
| **Tokuno Islands** | 1448 × 1448 | Eastern islands inspired by feudal Japan. |

The Felucca/Trammel landmass splits into **Britannia** (the mainland and home islands) and the
**T2A Lost Lands** (reached via dungeons and special passages).

## The eight major cities

Full pages, with services and nearby resources, for each:

- [Britain](/world/britain/) — the capital; every service under one (very large) roof
- [Minoc](/world/minoc/) — mining town in the north-east
- [Yew](/world/yew/) — forest town of lumberjacks and the Courts of Truth
- [Vesper](/world/vesper/) — canal-laced port and trading city
- [Trinsic](/world/trinsic/) — walled city of paladins
- [Jhelom](/world/jhelom/) — island city of warriors
- [Moonglow](/world/moonglow/) — island city of mages and the Lycaeum
- [Skara Brae](/world/skara-brae/) — island town of rangers and ranches

Other notable settlements (see the map for all 18): New Magincia, Nujel'm, Serpent's Hold,
Cove, Ocllo, Wind (mages only), Buccaneer's Den (no guards!), New Haven, and the Lost Lands
towns of Papua and Delucia.
