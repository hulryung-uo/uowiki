Vesper, the City of Industry, is built on islands and bridges — a prosperous, cosmopolitan
port that grew rich sitting between the Minoc mines and the open sea. Goods come down from the
mountains and go out by ship; merchants of every craft set up shop along the canals, and the
docks never quite sleep. Of all the great towns, Vesper feels the most like a working city:
crowded, mercantile, and proud of it. Where neighboring Minoc is a single-trade mining camp,
Vesper is the full-service marketplace that completes it.

## Getting there

Vesper sits on the **north-east** coast of the Felucca/Trammel mainland. The nearest public
gate is the **Minoc Moongate** (2701, 692), a short walk west; take any gate, choose Minoc,
and stroll east into Vesper. Boats and ships dock here too, and mages can
[*Recall*](/skills/magery/) to a marked rune. See
[Moongates & shrines](/world/moongates-and-shrines/).

## Who should come here

**Traders, fishers, and anyone working the Minoc loop who wants a full-service city.** Vesper
has the bank and the breadth of vendors that Minoc lacks, while staying close to the same
mountains. Sailors and fishermen launch from here; the dungeon **Covetous** lies just
south-west for hunters seeking more excitement than the docks provide.
