Vesper, the City of Industry, is built on islands and bridges — a prosperous port sitting
between the Minoc mines and the open sea, where trade is brisk and the docks never quite sleep.

## Who should come here

**Traders, fishers, and anyone working the Minoc loop who wants a full-service city.** Vesper
has the bank and the breadth of vendors that Minoc lacks, while staying close to the same
mountains. Sailors and fishermen launch from here; the dungeon **Covetous** lies just
south-west for hunters seeking more excitement than the docks provide.
