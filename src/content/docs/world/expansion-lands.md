Britannia is bigger than the old map. Beyond Felucca/Trammel and the earlier expansion facets
lie the **Stygian Abyss** and **Time of Legends** lands — the gargoyle homeland of **Ter Mur**
and the prehistoric **Valley of Eodon** — each with its own dungeons, creatures, and content.

## Ter Mur — the gargoyle homeland (Stygian Abyss)

**Ter Mur** is the land of the gargoyles. It's home to the **Royal City** (the gargish capital,
with its own services and the Royal City moongate/teleporters) and the entrance to the great
**Stygian Abyss** dungeon. Gargoyle characters belong here, and much SA-era content — including
several [peerless bosses](/playing/peerless-bosses/) and the [Stygian Dragon](/bestiary/bosses/) —
lives in and under Ter Mur.

**Getting there:** the classic route runs through the **Tomb of Kings** and the **Underworld**:

- **Tomb of Kings** — a puzzle passage of glowing **words of power** (Britannian runic gates)
  that links Britannia to Ter Mur; reciting/passing the correct words opens the way through.
- **Underworld** — a long subterranean cavern zone connecting the passage to Ter Mur, full of
  its own spawn.

The **Sacred Quest** (the *Quest of Singularity* at the Shrine of Singularity) is the gargoyle
rite of passage tied to this region.

## The Valley of Eodon (Time of Legends)

**Eodon** is a hidden prehistoric jungle valley — a land of **dinosaurs** (tyrannosaurs,
dragon turtles, and more), volcanic terrain, and warring native **tribes**. Its signature
content:

- **The Myrmidex Invasion** — a faction conflict between the valley's tribes and the insectoid
  **Myrmidex**; players pick a side and fight for it.
- **Shadowguard ("the Roof")** — a multi-wing **raid-style encounter** (a series of themed
  challenge rooms/bosses) that is some of the hardest grouped PvE on the shard.
- A web of **Eodon quests** (the Valley of One questline and others) introducing the tribes and
  the valley's dangers.

## Earlier expansion facets

The middle-era facets are also part of the world:

- **Malas** (Age of Shadows) — home of **Luna** and **Umbra**, and the **[Doom](/playing/doom-gauntlet/)**
  gauntlet dungeon.
- **Tokuno** (Samurai Empire) — the eastern islands, with their own dungeons and the
  **[Treasures of Tokuno](/playing/seasonal-events/)** event.
- **Ilshenar** — the no-recall facet, home of the **[paragons](/playing/paragons/)**.
