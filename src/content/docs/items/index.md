## Shard notes that affect items

- Items left on the ground decay in **60 minutes** (`Config/General.cfg`) — bank it or lose it.
- Loot quality uses the budget system with Felucca bonuses — see
  [server rules](/shard/server-rules/).
- Vendor stock refreshes hourly and prices respond to supply and demand
  (`Config/Vendors.cfg`).
