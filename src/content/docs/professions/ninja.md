## What this profession is

The ninja is a stealth fighter who strikes from the shadows. [Ninjitsu](/skills/ninjitsu/)
turns hiding into a weapon — ambush damage, decoys, short-range teleports, and animal forms
for travel and special abilities. On this shard Ninjitsu is real (the EJ expansion stack
includes Samurai-Empire-era skills). It pairs with a stealth dexxer or an archer for hit-and-fade play.

## Core skills

- [Ninjitsu](/skills/ninjitsu/) — the discipline; powers its abilities and forms.
- [Hiding](/skills/hiding/) and [Stealth](/skills/stealth/) — vanish on demand and move while hidden; the foundation ambush and escape depend on.
- A weapon skill — [Fencing](/skills/fencing/) (kama, sai) or [Swordsmanship](/skills/swordsmanship/); archers pair Ninjitsu with a bow instead.
- [Tactics](/skills/tactics/) and [Anatomy](/skills/anatomy/) for damage; [Healing](/skills/healing/) for bandages.

## The build

There is **no dedicated ninja template page** yet. Build it as a stealth dexxer with a Ninjitsu
slot: start from the [Warrior Template](/templates/warrior/) for the combat core, then add
Ninjitsu + Hiding + Stealth. A common spread is weapon + Tactics + Anatomy + Ninjitsu + Hiding +
Stealth + Healing. See [7x GM Templates](/templates/seven-gm/) for the 700-point budget — note
that Hiding + Stealth + Ninjitsu is a heavy three-slot investment.

## How to play it

Read [Hiding & Stealth](/playing/hiding-and-stealth/) first — it's the whole point. Then
[Combat Basics](/playing/combat-basics/) and [Combat Advanced](/playing/combat-advanced/) for
the swing loop and special moves; [Magic Schools](/playing/magic-schools/) places Ninjitsu among the schools.

The toolkit (all confirmed under `servuo: Scripts/Spells/Ninjitsu/`):

- **Animal Form** — turn into a creature for travel speed and form-specific abilities; the ninja's signature mobility.
- **Mirror Image** — spawn decoy clones that soak attacks.
- **Shadowjump** — short teleport between shadows to reposition or escape.
- **Surprise Attack** / **Backstab** — bonus damage when striking from hiding.
- **Death Strike** — a delayed wound that punishes a target for moving.
- **Ki Attack** / **Focus Attack** — momentum and focus-based hit bonuses.
- **Smoke bombs** (craftable items) hide you instantly even in combat; **shuriken** are thrown ranged strikes.

## Gear

- [Weapons](/items/weapons/) and the [weapon catalog](/items/catalog/weapons/) — fast Fencing weapons (kama, sai) suit ambush bursts; carry **shuriken** and **smoke bombs**.
- [Armor](/items/armor/) — light enough not to hinder stealth; balanced resists.
- [Magic item properties](/magic/) — Swing Speed, Hit leech, and Stealth/Hiding-friendly mods; carry **bandages**.

## Making a living

Ninjas farm by ambush — open from hiding for a big first hit, then fade and re-hide between
strikes to take less return damage. Animal Form speeds travel between farming spots. The
stealth kit also suits scouting and avoiding PvP. Sell loot via [Vendors & Banking](/playing/vendors-and-banking/).
