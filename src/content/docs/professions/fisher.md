## What this profession is

The fisher harvests the sea. With nothing but a fishing pole and a stretch of water you pull
up fish to cook and sell, the odd pair of boots, message-in-a-bottle SOS notes that lead to
sunken treasure, and rare big fish worth mounting as trophies. It is the most relaxed
gathering profession — low setup, low danger near shore — but it quietly feeds two others:
cooking turns the catch into food, and cartography turns SOS bottles into underwater treasure
hunts.

## Core skills

- [Fishing](/skills/fishing/) — the headline skill: cast a pole on water to catch fish, items, and SOS bottles.
- [Cooking](/skills/cooking/) — turn raw fish into cooked food worth selling and eating.
- [Cartography](/skills/cartography/) — decode the SOS and treasure maps fishing turns up, tying the catch into [treasure hunting](/playing/treasure-hunting/).

## The build

There is no dedicated fisher template yet — Fishing slots onto a gatherer or treasure-hunter
character. Pair it with Cooking and Cartography (and the treasure-hunting kit of Lockpicking
and a combat skill for guardians) and see [7x GM Templates](/templates/seven-gm/) for fitting
it all under the 700-point cap.

## How to gather

Read [Gathering Resources](/playing/gathering-resources/) for the fishing loop. Cooking the
catch is covered on the [Cooking crafting](/crafting/cooking/) page.

The loop: stand on or beside water, double-click a **fishing pole**, and target the water.
Each cast pulls up something — most often fish, sometimes boots or other junk, occasionally a
**message in a bottle (SOS)**, a **big fish**, or even treasure. Deeper ocean (from a boat)
yields better catches than shallow shore water. Cook the fish you catch into steaks for food;
decode SOS bottles with [Cartography](/skills/cartography/) to find a spot, then sail there
and fish or dive up the sunken chest.

## What you catch / tools

- [Tools](/items/tools/) — a **fishing pole** is the fisher's one essential tool; a fletcher or NPC can supply one.
- **Fish, big fish, and junk** — the everyday catch; raw fish become food via [Cooking](/crafting/cooking/).
- **Message in a bottle (SOS)** — leads to a sunken treasure spot; the gateway to [treasure hunting](/playing/treasure-hunting/) at sea.
- [Resources](/items/resources/) — special catches and reagents the sea occasionally yields.

## Making a living

Fishers earn by selling **cooked fish** as cheap, reliable food, mounting **big fish** as
trophies, and — the big payoff — cashing in **SOS loot**: the underwater chests an SOS leads
to hold gold, gems, and magic gear like a land treasure chest. Rare catches and message
bottles themselves also sell to other players. Move stock through
[Vendors & Banking](/playing/vendors-and-banking/).
