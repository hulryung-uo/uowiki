## What this profession is

The carpenter-bowyer works in wood. You chop logs from trees, mill them into boards, and
build furniture, house add-ons, musical instruments, and bows. The two crafts share a raw
material — boards — so it is natural to run Carpentry and Bowcraft together off a single
lumberjacking supply. Carpenters furnish and decorate houses; bowyers arm every archer on
the shard.

## Core skills

- [Lumberjacking](/skills/lumberjacking/) — chop logs from trees; the gathering end that feeds both crafts.
- [Carpentry](/skills/carpentry/) — build furniture, containers, house add-ons, and instruments from boards.
- [Bowcraft/Fletching](/skills/bowcraft-fletching/) — craft bows, crossbows, arrows, and bolts from boards and feathers.

## The build

Follow the [Lumberjack Template](/templates/lumberjack/) for the stat spread (Strength for
hauling logs), the skill order, and where to harvest at each stage. The natural loadout is
Lumberjacking + Carpentry + Bowcraft/Fletching plus combat or travel skills. For fitting
seven grandmaster skills under the 700-point cap, see [7x GM Templates](/templates/seven-gm/).

## How to craft / gather

Read [Gathering Resources](/playing/gathering-resources/) for the woodcutting loop and
[Crafting](/playing/crafting/) for the craft menu and exceptional mechanics. Recipes live on
the [Carpentry crafting](/crafting/carpentry/) and [Bowfletching crafting](/crafting/bowfletching/)
pages.

The loop: equip a hatchet or axe and chop trees for logs, then mill the logs into boards at
a sawmill or with a saw. From there, open the appropriate tool's menu and build. Carpentry
needs only boards for most items; fletching needs boards plus feathers (looted from birds)
for arrows. Higher skill yields exceptional, stronger output.

## What you make / tools

- [Tools](/items/tools/) — a **saw** for carpentry and **fletcher's tools** for bows; a tinker can supply both.
- [Resources](/items/resources/) — **logs and boards**, the wood chain that feeds every recipe.
- **Furniture and house add-ons** — tables, chairs, and containers for [decorating](/playing/decorating/) the [house types](/playing/house-types/) players own.
- [Instruments catalog](/items/catalog/instruments/) — lutes, harps, and drums for bards.
- [Weapons](/items/weapons/) and the [weapon catalog](/items/catalog/weapons/) — **bows and crossbows** for archers.

## Making a living

Bowyers sell **bows and crossbows** to NPC bowyer shops for reliable gold and to archers for
exceptional pieces. Carpenters sell **boards** in bulk to other woodworkers, **kegs** to
alchemists for storing potions, and **furniture** to homeowners decorating their houses.
Move stock through [Vendors & Banking](/playing/vendors-and-banking/).
