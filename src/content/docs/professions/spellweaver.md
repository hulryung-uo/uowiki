## What this profession is

The spellweaver (Arcanist) is a support and utility caster built on [Spellweaving](/skills/spellweaving/),
a Mondain's-Legacy-era school. On this shard Spellweaving is real (the EJ expansion stack
includes ML skills). It's rarely a character's only school — it layers onto a [mage](/professions/mage/),
[mystic](/professions/mystic/), or [tamer](/professions/tamer/) to add heals, buffs, summons, and area damage.

## Core skills

- [Spellweaving](/skills/spellweaving/) — the casting skill; sets spell power and the strength of the Arcane Focus you can hold.
- A primary school to build around — [Magery](/skills/magery/) + [Evaluating Intelligence](/skills/evaluating-intelligence/), [Mysticism](/skills/mysticism/) + [Focus](/skills/focus/), or pet skills for a [tamer](/professions/tamer/).
- [Meditation](/skills/meditation/) and [Resisting Spells](/skills/resisting-spells/) to fuel and survive.

## The Arcane Circle mechanic

Spellweaving's signature is the **Arcane Circle**. Standing in one (cast where other weavers
gather) grants an **Arcane Focus** that empowers your weaving spells — and **the more
spellweavers nearby, the stronger the focus**, so it's a group-friendly school. *Arcane
Empowerment* further boosts the strength/duration of your next weave. Both are confirmed:
`servuo: Scripts/Spells/Spellweaving/ArcaneCircle.cs` and `ArcaneEmpowerment.cs`. Solo, you
still get a focus from items (an arcane robe/gem), just a weaker one.

## The build

There is **no dedicated spellweaver template page** yet. Treat Spellweaving as one slot added to
a caster: start from the [Mage Template](/templates/mage/) (or a tamer/mystic spread), keep the
high-Int caster stats, and fit [Spellweaving](/skills/spellweaving/) in. See
[7x GM Templates](/templates/seven-gm/) for the 700-point math.

## How to play it

[Magic Schools](/playing/magic-schools/) places Spellweaving among the schools; [Spellcasting](/playing/spellcasting/)
covers casting and [Meditation & Mana](/playing/meditation-and-mana/) covers mana.

The toolkit (all confirmed under `servuo: Scripts/Spells/Spellweaving/`):

- **Gift of Renewal** — a heal-over-time buff; **Gift of Life** is a delayed self/ally resurrection insurance.
- **Arcane Empowerment** — boost your next weave; **Attune Weapon** absorbs incoming damage.
- **Summon Fey** / **Summon Fiend** — conjured combat allies; **Nature's Fury** summons an aggressive swarm.
- **Wildfire** / **Thunderstorm** — persistent area damage for clearing packs.
- **Word of Death** — heavy damage to a weakened target.
- **Reaper Form** — a self-transform trading speed for spell power.

## Gear

- [Magic item properties](/magic/) — the **arcane robe/focus** items that grant a solo focus; plus **Spell Damage**, **Lower Mana Cost**, and **Faster Casting** for the spells.
- [Armor](/items/armor/) — mage-friendly with balanced resists.
- Spellweaving uses an **Arcane Focus** charge rather than the eight mage reagents for many spells; carry focus items.

## Making a living

A spellweaver rarely farms alone on Spellweaving — it amplifies whatever your main school or pet
already does: heal and buff yourself, drop Wildfire on a pack, and finish with Word of Death.
In groups the Arcane Circle makes everyone's weaves stronger. Sell loot via
[Vendors & Banking](/playing/vendors-and-banking/).
