Bowcraft/Fletching (Fletching) shapes boards and feathers into bows, crossbows, and ammunition, the supply line behind every archer. Use a fletching kit on boards; higher skill unlocks heavier ranged weapons and improves quality. Train by fletching the highest-difficulty item you can reliably make.
