Anatomy is a passive support skill: studying a creature's physiology raises your melee damage and improves how much Healing restores. It pairs with Tactics on warriors and with Healing on bandage medics. Train it by using the skill on yourself or others to read their physical condition, or simply let combat and healing carry it.
