Necromancy casts a school of dark spells — curses, summons, transformations, and damage — using necromantic reagents such as bat wing and grave dust. On this shard it pairs with Spirit Speak, which boosts several of its effects. It is a Mastery skill. Train by casting the toughest necromancy spell you can reliably afford.
