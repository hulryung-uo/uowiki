Animal Lore lets you appraise a creature, revealing its hit points, stats, resistances, loyalty, and whether it can be tamed. It is essential support for tamers, who use it alongside Animal Taming and Veterinary to evaluate and care for pets. Train by loring animals repeatedly; tougher creatures grant gains at higher skill.
