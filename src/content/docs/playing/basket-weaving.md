**Basket weaving** is a small *Stygian Abyss*-era **decorative craft**: you weave woven
**baskets** — purely decorative containers — from **dry reeds**. It's a gardening-adjacent
hobby rather than a combat or economy skill.

## Getting started

1. **Learn the craft** from a **Basket Weaving book** — double-click it to learn (it teaches
   permanently; `pm.BasketWeaving = true`). The game notes you'll *"need gardeners to make
   reeds out of plants."*
2. **Get dry reeds.** Reeds come from the [gardening / plant system](/playing/gardening/) —
   plants are turned into **dry reeds** (`DryReeds`), the raw material for weaving.
3. **Weave baskets** from the reeds using the basket-weaving menu, producing a range of
   decorative basket styles.

## Coloring

Baskets can be **dyed** using the plant-based dyes the same system provides — **plant
pigments** and **natural dye** (fixed with color fixative) — so you can make baskets in the
full range of [plant hues](/playing/gardening/) to match a décor theme.

## Why do it

It's a **decorator's and gardener's pastime** — the baskets are sought-after [house
décor](/playing/decorating/), and the craft gives a use for the reeds your garden produces.
Niche, but a satisfying loop for players invested in [gardening](/playing/gardening/) and
[decorating](/playing/decorating/).
