The **Ultima Store** is the in-game catalog of cosmetic and convenience items, enabled on this
shard (`UltimaStore.Enabled`). You browse it from the store interface, spend **Sovereigns** (the
store's premium currency), and claim what you buy.

## Browsing and buying

The store is organized into **categories** (`StoreCategory`) — for example **Featured**,
**Character**, **Equipment**, and more — each holding a set of **store entries** (`StoreEntry`)
with a Sovereign price. Pick an item, pay its Sovereign cost, and it's delivered to your
**pending items** to claim into your pack.

## Sovereigns

**Sovereigns** are the store currency, held on your account. How you acquire them is a
**shard-level / account matter** (set by the server operator) rather than something earned
through normal play — check the shard's own info for how to get them here.

## What's on offer

Store items are typically **cosmetic or convenience** — house décor, character customization,
storage and quality-of-life items — rather than raw power. It's the "extras" shop, separate
from the gold economy and the [Community Collections](/playing/community-collections/) /
[Clean Up Britannia](/playing/cleanup-britannia/) reward tracks.
