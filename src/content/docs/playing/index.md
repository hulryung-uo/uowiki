## The basics, in one breath

- **To attack:** hold a weapon, switch to **war mode**, and double-click (or target) the
  enemy. Your character closes in and swings automatically. See [Combat basics](/playing/combat-basics/).
- **To heal:** double-click a **bandage**, then target yourself (or a hurt ally/pet). After
  a few seconds the wound closes. See [Healing](/playing/healing/).
- **To move:** click where you want to go; click farther to run (costs stamina). To cross
  the map, **Recall** or **Gate** to a marked rune. See [Movement & travel](/playing/movement-and-travel/).
- **To cast:** carry a **spellbook** and **reagents**, invoke the spell, and target. See
  [Spellcasting](/playing/spellcasting/).
