Beyond the eight [virtues](/playing/virtues/), your character tracks **loyalty ratings** — your
standing with various groups, factions, and systems around Britannia. You check them from the
**loyalty-rating menu** (`LoyaltyRating`, a context-menu entry that opens the
`LoyaltyRatingGump`).

## What it tracks

The loyalty gump gathers your reputation across the shard's point/loyalty systems in one place —
for example your standing in **[city loyalty](/playing/city-loyalty/)**, event-based loyalties
like **[Treasures of Tokuno](/playing/seasonal-events/)**, the **[Clean Up Britannia](/playing/cleanup-britannia/)**
exchange, and other faction reputations. Each is a running score raised by acting in that
group's interest.

## Why it matters

Loyalty standings **gate rewards and access**: higher standing with a given system unlocks its
titles, lets you buy its rewards, or opens its content. The rating menu is simply the dashboard
where you see all of those scores at once, so you know how close you are to the next reward
tier in each.
