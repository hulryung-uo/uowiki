**Ninjitsu** is the *Samurai Empire* ninja discipline — stealth and trickery arts that use
**mana** (no reagents), invoked from the **Book of Ninjitsu**. It's built on
**[Hiding](/skills/hiding/)** and **[Stealth](/skills/stealth/)**: many of its strikes are far
stronger (or only work) **while hidden**, and **smoke bombs** let a ninja vanish mid-fight.
Governed by the **[Ninjitsu](/skills/ninjitsu/)** skill.

## Abilities

| Ability | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Animal Form** | Shapeshift into an animal (wolf, cat, rat, giant rat, etc.) — fast travel, stealthier movement, and form-specific bonuses. |
| **Mirror Image** | Spawn **decoy clones** of yourself that soak enemy hits and split their attacks. |
| **Shadowjump** | Short-range **teleport** while hidden, to reposition unseen. |
| **Focus Attack** | Buff your next hit with bonus damage and leech (costs stamina). |
| **Ki Attack** | Bonus-damage charge attack when you close from a distance. |
| **Surprise Attack** | From hiding: bonus damage that also **lowers the target's defenses**. |
| **Backstab** | A heavy strike delivered **out of hiding** — the ninja's opener. |
| **Death Strike** | Place a delayed mark; it detonates for burst damage when the target **moves** (more if they move a lot). |

A ninja weaves **Hiding/Stealth → Backstab/Surprise Attack** for opening burst, uses **Mirror
Image** and **smoke bombs** to survive, and **Animal Form** to travel and reposition.
