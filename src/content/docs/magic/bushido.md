**Bushido** is the *Samurai Empire* warrior discipline — a set of **combat arts** rather than
spells. They use **mana** (no reagents), are invoked from the **Book of Bushido**, and are built
around **[Parrying](/skills/parrying/)** (Bushido massively boosts weapon-parry block chance)
and the **[Honor](/playing/virtues/)** virtue's *Perfection* bonus. Governed by the
**[Bushido](/skills/bushido/)** skill.

## Abilities

| Ability | Skill | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Confidence** | 25 | Defensive stance — heals you over the next few seconds, more if you successfully defend. |
| **Counter Attack** | 40 | Riposte: your next successful **parry** triggers an immediate counter-hit. |
| **Evasion** | 60 | Greatly raises your block/avoid chance for a short window. |
| **Lightning Strike** | ~50 | Your next swing is a guaranteed, faster hit with bonus accuracy. |
| **Momentum Strike** | ~70 | Strike your target *and* a second nearby enemy at once. |
| **Honorable Execution** | ~25 | A finisher: big damage, and on a kill it wipes your penalties/stat-loss and boosts you — but leaves you exposed if it fails. |

Bushido shines on a **sword-and-board parry build**: with Bushido + a shield, your parry rate is
far higher than Parrying alone (see [Advanced combat → Parrying](/playing/combat-advanced/#parrying)),
and **Confidence/Evasion/Counter Attack** turn defense into sustain and counter-damage.
