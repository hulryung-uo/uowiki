**Chivalry** is the paladin's holy magic (AOS era). It needs **no reagents** — instead it runs
on **tithing points**, which you gain by **donating gold at a shrine** ("I wish to tithe"),
plus a little **mana**. Abilities are cast from the **Book of Chivalry** and scale with the
**[Chivalry](/skills/chivalry/)** skill (and your **Karma**, which makes holy effects stronger).

## Abilities

| Ability | Mana | Tithing | Skill | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Cleanse by Fire** | 10 | 10 | 5 | Burns away poison from a target (cures), with minor self-damage. |
| **Close Wounds** | 10 | 10 | 0 | Heal a target (no bandages needed). |
| **Consecrate Weapon** | 10 | 10 | 15 | Your weapon strikes the target's *weakest* resist for a time. |
| **Dispel Evil** | 10 | 10 | 35 | Drive off / dispel undead and summoned evil. |
| **Divine Fury** | 15 | 10 | 25 | Combat rage: faster swing/regen, slight defense drop. |
| **Enemy of One** | 20 | 10 | 45 | Bonus damage vs one creature type (you take more from others). |
| **Holy Light** | 10 | 10 | 55 | Area light-damage burst around you. |
| **Noble Sacrifice** | 20 | 30 | 65 | Fully heal/cure/resurrect nearby allies — at the cost of your own life. |
| **Remove Curse** | 20 | 10 | 5 | Strips curses and negative effects from a target. |
| **Sacred Journey** | 10 | 15 | 15 | The paladin's travel spell (recall/gate equivalent, via rune). |

## Tithing

You can't cast Chivalry without **tithing points**. Visit any **shrine** and offer gold to
convert it into tithing points (stored on your character). Each ability spends a set number of
points per cast, so keep your tithing topped up.
