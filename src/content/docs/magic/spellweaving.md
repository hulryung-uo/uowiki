**Spellweaving** (the *Mondain's Legacy* arcanist school) is nature/elf magic that uses **no
reagents** — only **mana** — and is governed by the **[Spellweaving](/skills/spellweaving/)**
skill. Its signature twist is the **Arcane Circle**: weaving inside one, especially with **other
spellweavers** present, **amplifies** your spells (more weavers = stronger effects), making it a
group-synergy school.

## Spells

| Spell | Mana | Skill | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Arcane Circle** | 24 | 0 | Form/join an Arcane Circle that empowers nearby weavers. |
| **Attune Weapon** | 24 | 0 | A damage-absorbing shield on yourself. |
| **Gift of Renewal** | 24 | 0 | Heal-over-time buff on a target. |
| **Nature's Fury** | 24 | 0 | Summon a swarm that harasses a foe. |
| **Immolating Weapon** | 32 | 10 | Your weapon adds fire damage for a number of hits. |
| **Thunderstorm** | 32 | 10 | Area energy burst that also slows casting. |
| **Ethereal Voyage** | 32 | 24 | Temporary "unnoticed by monsters" travel state. |
| **Arcane Empowerment** | 50 | 24 | Boosts your next spells' power/duration. |
| **Reaper Form** | 34 | 24 | Transform into a reaper (spell-damage buff, can't move). |
| **Summon Fey** | 10 | 38 | Summon a fey ally (defensive pet). |
| **Summon Fiend** | 10 | 38 | Summon a fiend ally (offensive pet). |
| **Gift of Life** | 70 | 38 | Auto-resurrect buff — revives the target on death. |
| **Dryad Allure** | 40 | 52 | Charm a creature to your side temporarily. |
| **Essence of Wind** | 40 | 52 | Cold area burst that slows and saps stamina. |
| **Wildfire** | 50 | 66 | Lingering fire field over an area. |
| **Word of Death** | 50 | 83 | Massive damage to a target — far deadlier the lower its health. |

Higher-tier weaves (**Gift of Life**, **Word of Death**) and a full **Arcane Circle** make a
weaver a powerful group support/finisher.
