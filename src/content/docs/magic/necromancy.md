**Necromancy** is the AOS-era dark magic school. Necromancers curse their enemies, drain life,
raise the dead, and shape-shift into monstrous **forms**. Spells cost **mana** and **reagents**
(the dark reagents — grave dust, daemon blood, nox crystal, pig iron, bat wing), are cast from a
**necromancer's spellbook**, and scale with the **[Necromancy](/skills/necromancy/)** skill;
**[Spirit Speak](/skills/spirit-speak/)** boosts many of their effects.

## Spells

| Spell | Mana | Skill | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Curse Weapon** | 7 | 0 | Your weapon leeches life on hit for a short time. |
| **Pain Spike** | 5 | 20 | Quick direct damage that fades partly back. |
| **Blood Oath** | 13 | 20 | Curse a foe so the damage *it* deals reflects back onto it. |
| **Corpse Skin** | 11 | 20 | Lowers a target's fire & poison resistance. |
| **Evil Omen** | 11 | 20 | The target's next harmful effect hits harder. |
| **Wraith Form** | 17 | 20 | Become a wraith — move through creatures, leech mana on hit. |
| **Mind Rot** | 17 | 30 | Raises the target's mana cost / drains mana over time. |
| **Summon Familiar** | 17 | 30 | Summon a necro pet familiar (horde minion, dark wolf, etc.). |
| **Horrific Beast** | 11 | 40 | Transform into a fast, regenerating beast (melee form). |
| **Animate Dead** | 23 | 40 | Raise a corpse as an undead minion. |
| **Poison Strike** | 17 | 50 | Poison-damage burst with splash to nearby foes. |
| **Wither** | 23 | 60 | Cold-damage area pulse around you. |
| **Strangle** | 29 | 65 | Damage-over-time that worsens as the target's stamina drops. |
| **Lich Form** | 23 | 70 | Become a lich — faster mana regen and casting, weaker to fire. |
| **Exorcism** | 40 | 80 | Banish/disrupt undead and channeling. |
| **Vengeful Spirit** | 41 | 80 | Summon a vengeful spirit that hunts a target. |
| **Vampiric Embrace** | 23 | 99 | Become a vampire — strong life leech, weakness to fire. |

The **forms** (Wraith / Horrific Beast / Lich / Vampiric Embrace) are mutually exclusive
transformations that define a necromancer's playstyle.
