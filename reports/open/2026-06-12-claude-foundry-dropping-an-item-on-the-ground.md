- page: src/content/docs/playing/items-and-inventory.md
- observed: On this AOS-era shard, a world drop targeted at the dropper's OWN tile is rejected server-side: PlayerMobile.OnDroppedItemToWorld refuses drops onto any tile occupied by a mobile in the z-band [destZ, destZ+16) — including the dropper — and Item.Bounce silently returns the item to the backpack with no message. Field-verified: foundry miner evals repeatedly 'dropped' ore at their feet, the ore bounced back to the pack every time, and the agents' ground-pile bookkeeping went phantom ('pile empty at target' on every collect attempt; run c16f4 cycles 1/3/4 agent logs, data/eval_logs/agent-evoc16f4c1s* etc.; servuo Scripts/Mobiles/PlayerMobile.cs:703-724)
- expected-per-wiki: The Items & Inventory page should warn that ground drops onto your own tile silently bounce back into the pack on AOS+ rules — step one tile away (or drop onto an existing ground stack) if you genuinely want the item on the ground
